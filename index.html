Frequently Asked Questions

How do I purchase premium topics?
To unlock premium topics, simply tap on any locked topic and follow the in-app purchase process.
Will I lose my purchased topics if I change devices?
No. Your purchases are tied to your Apple ID. Simply restore purchases on your new device to access your premium topics.
How often do you add new conversation starters?
We regularly update our content with fresh conversation starters. Make sure to keep your app updated to access the latest content.
Can I save my favorite conversation starters?
Yes! Tap the heart icon next to any conversation starter to save it to your favorites for quick access.
I'm having trouble with a purchase
If your purchase isn't showing up, first try the "Restore Purchases" button in the app settings. If the issue persists, please contact our support team.
