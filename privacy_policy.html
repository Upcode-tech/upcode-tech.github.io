Last Updated: November 3, 2024

Introduction

Welcome to Smoooth ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy.

This privacy policy describes our practices for maintaining user privacy while using our app. We believe in complete transparency regarding our privacy practices.

Information We Don't Collect

We want to be clear: Smoooth does not collect, store, or process any personal information. We believe in providing a private and secure experience for our users. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Personal identification information
  • Location data
  • Usage statistics
  • Device information
  • Cookies or tracking data
  • Chat or conversation history

App Store Purchases

When you make purchases through the App Store:

  • All transactions are handled directly by Apple through the App Store
  • We never have access to your payment information
  • Purchase records are maintained by Apple according to their privacy policy

Data Storage

All app content (conversation starters and topics) is stored locally on your device. We do not maintain any user-specific data on our servers.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect or maintain information from persons under 13 years of age. If you are under 13, please do not use the app without parental guidance.

Changes to Our Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.
